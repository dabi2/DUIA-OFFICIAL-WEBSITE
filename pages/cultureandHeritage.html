Experience the authentic rural lifestyle in exclusive, untouched villages. Immerse yourself in the daily lives of local communities, savor their traditional cuisine, and stay at cozy homestays or travelers' nests. Explore the village with a knowledgeable local guide.

MAWPHLANG LAWKYNTANG

Meghalaya Lawkyntang, also known as sacred groves, are revered sanctuaries of nature deeply ingrained in the cultural fabric of Meghalaya. These pristine forests, left untouched by human intervention, serve as sacred sites where ancient rituals and traditions are preserved. Each Lawkyntang is a haven of biodiversity, housing rare flora and fauna, and embodying the harmonious relationship between the Khasi people and their natural surroundings. These sacred groves stand as living testaments to the indigenous conservation practices and spiritual beliefs of the region, offering visitors a glimpse into the profound connection between culture, ecology, and reverence for the Earth.

NARTIANG MEGALITHIC PARK

Nartiang Megalithic Park, nestled in the serene landscapes of Meghalaya, stands as a testament to the ancient heritage of the Khasi people. This archaeological marvel showcases a collection of ancient monoliths, believed to be erected centuries ago by tribal communities for various ceremonial and ritualistic purposes. Steeped in history and mystery, the park offers visitors a glimpse into the cultural significance and craftsmanship of these imposing stone structures, making it a fascinating destination for history enthusiasts and curious travelers alike.

NARTIANG DURGA TEMPLE

The Nartiang Durga Temple stands as a testament to Meghalaya's rich cultural heritage. Located in the quaint village of Nartiang, this ancient temple is dedicated to Goddess Durga, revered by locals and visitors alike. Constructed with traditional architecture and adorned with intricate carvings, the temple offers a glimpse into the region's religious significance and architectural prowess. As one of the oldest temples in the state, Nartiang Durga Temple continues to be a place of worship and pilgrimage, drawing devotees seeking blessings and spiritual solace amidst its serene surroundings.
