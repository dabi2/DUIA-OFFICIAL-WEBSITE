Experience adrenaline-fueled adventures with our Meghalaya Adventure 
Tours. From treks through lush forests to thrilling canyoning expeditions, 
each promises heart-pounding excitement amidst breathtaking landscapes.

CAVING

Journey on an exhilarating underground odyssey with Maw Mluh Caving, where the secrets of Meghalaya's hidden world come to life. Venture deep into ancient limestone caves, hailed as a geo heritage site, where every turn unveils breathtaking rock formations and mysterious chambers waiting to be discovered. Of special note, the fossil unearthed within Maw Mluh Cave played a pivotal role in determining the Meghalayan age, adding an intriguing layer of historical intrigue to our journey.

CANYONING

Canyoning, is the thrilling activity of exploring canyons by navigating through a combination of activities such as hiking, scrambling, rappelling, swimming, jumping and sometimes even diving. In Mawlyngbna, this adventure takes on a whole new dimension as participants traverse through beautiful gorges, plunge the beautiful pools and slide down thrilling cascades.

CAMPING

Camping in Meghalaya's Umpung and Shnongpdeng villages offers a serene retreat amidst lush greenery and pristine rivers. As the sun sets, the tranquil ambiance is complemented by the soothing sounds of nature. Under a blanket of stars, campers gather around crackling campfires, fostering connections and sharing stories. Waking up to birdsong and the gentle flow of the river, each day promises new adventures, from hiking through lush forests to kayaking along clear waters. It's an enriching escape into nature's embrace.

WATERFALL RAPELLING

Located just three hours from Shillong in Syntung village, the hidden gem of Shonghang waterfall awaits. This 475-meter gorge boasts a breathtaking cascade carved by millennia of flowing water. Upper Shonghang presents a thrilling challenge with a 40ft rappel, including a daunting 25ft overhang. With expertise in swift water techniques, adventurers can navigate this technical canyon. Yet, plans are underway to offer an alternative exit route before the waterfall, making this exhilarating experience accessible to all tourists, regardless of rappelling experience.
