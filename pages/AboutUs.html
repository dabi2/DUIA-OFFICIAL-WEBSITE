ABOUT US

With over 11 years of experience, Gerald Samuel Duia transitioned from being a tour guide to founding Duia

Trailblazers in March 2018. Focused on providing the authentic Meghalayan experience, the company specializes in

Eco Rural, Culture & Heritage, and Adventure Tours. Registered with the Meghalaya Tourism Department, Duia

Trailblazers works closely with local communities, offering curated experiences while promoting sustainable tourism

practices. They've organized a variety of tours and events, including camping, trekking, and capacity-building

programs, contributing to both local livelihoods and conservation efforts.
